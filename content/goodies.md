As a certified nerd™, I spend a big part of my life in the terminal, so I run lots of configurations. All managed with [GNU Stow](https://www.gnu.org/software/stow/) in my [dotfiles](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles). I will eventually get to [Nix](https://nixos.org/), but haven't had the need yet.

## Editor

- [Neovim](https://neovim.io/) — In an effort to run everything as OSS as possible, I am shifting from VSCode to Neovim. Almost covers all features I use daily. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/neovim)

## Terminal

- [Ghostty](https://ghostty.org/) — Minimalistic and fast, speaks to me.
- [zsh](https://www.zsh.org/) — A classic that needs no introduction. With oh-my-zsh, fzf-tab, zoxide... [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/zsh)
- [tmux](https://github.com/tmux/tmux) — One of my latest additions, love at first `brew`. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/tmux)
- [Starship](https://starship.rs/) — Some ricing is always nice. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/starship)

## macOS

- [AeroSpace](https://github.com/nikitabobko/AeroSpace) — Can't imagine my workflow without a tiling window manager. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/aerospace)
- [SketchyBar](https://github.com/FelixKratz/SketchyBar) + [JankyBorders](https://github.com/FelixKratz/JankyBorders) — More ricing, integrated with Aerospace. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/sketchybar)

## Other tools

- [lazygit](https://github.com/jesseduffield/lazygit) — My favorite to run inside neovim, but currently looking into [jj](https://github.com/jj-vcs/jj).
- [k9s](https://k9scli.io/) — Just ergonomic TUI K8s navigation. [config](https://github.com/pausic/.dotfiles/tree/master/k9s)
- [flux9s](https://github.com/dgunzy/flux9s) — Like k9s but for FluxCD.
- [mise](https://mise.jdx.dev/) — For environment management.
